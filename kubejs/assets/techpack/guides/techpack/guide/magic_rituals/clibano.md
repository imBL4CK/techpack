# <Color id="blue">What is clibano?</Color>
The clibano is a magical furnace that has its own recipes. The clibano was developed by one of the legendary wizards who made it to process large quantities of diamonds from the ancient king.

When heating raw ores in the clibano, there is a chance of generating residues. This residues becomes an item when it reaches 100% quantity.

## The clibano can be automated like a furnace.
- The top face is the input for the ingredients
- The side face is the input for the fuel
- The bottom face is the output for the recipe

# <Color id="blue">How can they be obtained?</Color>
The clibano is a multi-block structure. You can press <Color id="blue">W</Color> to see its ponder.

It is worth remembering that to transform the structure into clibano, it is necessary to click on the <ItemLink id="forbidden_arcanus:clibano_core" /> with a <ItemLink id="forbidden_arcanus:mundabitur_dust" />

# <Color id="blue">What are they for?</Color>
It can be used to process:

- Raw Ores, generating residues
- Native Clusters, generating 4 outputs
- Cinnabar, generating Quicksilver
- Soul and, generating Soul Glass
- etc...

<Color id="green">⚠ Tip</Color>: To see more uses, press U or right-click on the JEI/EMI interface
