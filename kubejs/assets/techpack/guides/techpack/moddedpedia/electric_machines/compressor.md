# <Color id="blue">Compressor</Color>
</Row>
A Compressor is a machine that has a powerful press capable of transforming basic materials into compressed variants. Unlike a metal former, the force of the press is not calibrated, therefore its uses are more specific.

* requires electrical energy to operate.
