# <Color id="blue">Welcome to Techpack!</Color>

Techpack is an expert modpack inspired by [GTNH](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/gt-new-horizons), [E2E](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/enigmatica2expert), and [E2EE](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/enigmatica-2-expert-extended) with the premise of build the greateful technologies that the human being can imagine, explore the galaxy, made a inclible magic rituals and more!

# <Color id="blue">What is a Moddedpedia?</Color>

Moddedpedia is a book that will help guide you on your adventure through Techpack!

* In this book, you will find a lot of interesting information that can complement the quests
* You will also find some hidden features of items
