## <Color id="yellow">What is Buffer?</Color>
It's also worth mentioning the term "Buffer," which will be used later. Simply put, a buffer refers to internal storage which depending on the context can maintained in the event of a power outage, for example.

This term is often used to refer to batteries and tanks, but it can also be commonly used to describe how much energy storage a machine can have.
