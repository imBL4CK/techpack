# First Days

<GameScene zoom="2">
<ImportStructure src="../game_scenes/first_days_build.nbt" />
</GameScene>

The first few days in the modpack must be spent wisely. It's recommended that you find an area to settle down, preferably a flat spot. A good food supply and planting crops will help you keep your progress going while grinding, so hunt as many animals as possible.

<Row>
<ItemImage id="supplementaries:sack" scale="3"/>
</Row>

Once you have a good amount of items and your inventory starts to fill up, craft an <ItemLink id="minecraft:bundle" /> or an <ItemLink id="supplementaries:sack" /> to make it easier to manage your inventory.

<Row>
<ItemImage id="supplementaries:flax" scale="3"/>
<ItemImage id="minecraft:wheat" scale="3"/>
</Row>

Planting <ItemLink id="supplementaries:flax" /> will guarantee a source of thread and <ItemLink id="supplementaries:sack" />. <ItemLink id="minecraft:wheat" /> is also essential for survival, since <ItemLink id="minecraft:bread" /> is one of the easiest foods to obtain and most efficient for cooking other dishes.
