# <Color id="blue">Crops</Color>
Crops are a good source of food and strictly necessary for cooking better recipes. Crops can generally be planted on <ItemLink id="minecraft:farmland" />, <ItemLink id="farmersdelight:rich_soil_farmland" /> and  <ItemLink id="supplementaries:planter" /> (in some cases it may be different) and grow over time.

# <Color id="blue">Planting and Growth</Color>

## <Color id="yellow">Normal Crops</Color>
- Crops need light to grow and adjacent water within a 4-block radius.
- When fully grown, they can be harvested with <Color id="yellow">Right-Click</Color>, thus performing a gentle-harvest (collecting the ready food and keeping the seed planted).

## <Color id="yellow">Rice</Color>
* Rice needs to be planted **submerged in water** (similar to kelp).
* After full growth, the player can harvest <ItemLink id="farmersdelight:rice_panicle" /> by clicking on the top block.

## <Color id="yellow">Tomato</Color>
* It is planted directly in **farmland** and grows like any other crop in the game.
* When a <ItemLink id="farmersdelight:rope" /> is placed above the crop, the tomato grows as a **climbing plant**, increasing the size of the crop.
