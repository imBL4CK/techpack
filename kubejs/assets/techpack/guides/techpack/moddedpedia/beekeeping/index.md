# Beekeeping
Beekeeping, or apiculture, is the practice of human-maintained bee colonies, typically in man-made hives. Beekeepers manage these colonies for various reasons, including harvesting honey and other hive products like beeswax and pollen

Beekeeping practices can be very useful for obtaining natural resources sustainably. The initial bee species are found by world generation; they are:

* Common
* Boreal
* Fossil
* Enigmatic
