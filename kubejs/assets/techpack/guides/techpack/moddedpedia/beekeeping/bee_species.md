# Bee Species
Bees are flying insects known for their crucial role in pollination, their ability to produce honey, and their social or solitary lifestyles. Abelhas possuem duas classes, drones ou queens, sendo drones os trabalhadores e queens as abelhas que dao as ordens.

Bees can be very useful as they generate organic resources that could otherwise be a hindrance in gameplay. It's important to remember that they are living beings, so they have a healthpoints (durability), an activity period, and a block they pollinate.

Bees reproduce in <ItemLink id="techpack:bee_house"/> and work in <ItemLink id="techpack:apiary"/>, check those other pages for more information.
