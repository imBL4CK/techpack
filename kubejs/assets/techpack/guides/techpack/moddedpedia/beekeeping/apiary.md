# <Color id="blue">Apiary</Color>

An apiary is the bees' work environment, where they produce combs, spending their time there.

It requires a queen and a drone to begin producing combs and <ItemLink id="techpack:beewax"/>, in addition to activity period and a pollinating block adjacent to apiary.
