# <Color id="blue">Bee House</Color>
</Row>
A bee house is a pleasant environment for bees, where they can reproduce and replicate their species.

It requires a drone and a queen to begin reproduction, as well as the species' activity period and a pollinating block adjacent to the beehouse.
